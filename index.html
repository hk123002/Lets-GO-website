Ticketing pricing

LetsGo's ticketing fees are set at the competitive rate of 3.7% + $1.30 (per ticket) plus Stripe Processing Fee of 2.9% + .30 (per transaction) for handling. Event hosts have the option to pass along fees, cover the ticketing fees or the entire fee, by ticket type. There is no charge for free events.

How does check-in work?

Students simply open the app and click on 'Check-in' to automatically check in at the event. There's no need for scanning or additional steps.

How does offline check-in work?

During offline check-in, once the internet is reconnected, the app communicates with the server to confirm the user's check-in. Our robust session management ensures no misuse of this feature, preventing unauthorized event access.

How does a university manage an event?

Universities have access to a dedicated admin portal, where they can comprehensively manage all aspects of events, including creation, editing, deletion, and financial transactions.
